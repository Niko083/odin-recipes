Jalapeno Steak

A steak dinner to die for!

Jalepenos mixed with the other ingrediants give a spicy and delicious kick to grilled steak. Marinate this steak in the fridge for up to 24 hours before cooking.

The Ingredients
  • 4 jalapeno peppers,steamed
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cracked black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 pound top sirloin steak
Steps
  1. Combine jalapenos, garlic, pepper, salt, lime juice and oregano in blender until smooth.
  2. Place steak in shallow pan or large resealabe bag. Pour jalapeno marinade over steak, and turn to coat. place in refrigerator and marinate for 8 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat grill on high heat and lighty oil the grill grate
  4. Drain and discard marinade. Grill steak 5 minutes per side , or to desired doneness.
