Baked Chicken

Glazed and Baked

Roast Chicken pieces with sticky, sweet and tangy coating

Ingredients
  • 3 pounds chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Place chicken in 9x13 inch baking dish. Mix together the oil, soy sauce, ketchup, honey, galic salt and pepper. Pour over chicken.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for one hour, or until sauce is caramelized
